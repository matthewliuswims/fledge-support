---

Open **Schedule** from the sidebar, click an empty slot, pick the client, set the duration, and save. Recurring sessions, rescheduling, and cancellations all live in this same flow.

We're writing the longer version (recurrence rules, holiday handling, calendar export). For now, if you hit a scheduling edge case — overlapping appointments, time-zone shifts, recurrence that won't behave — email <a href="mailto:admin@fledgepractice.com">admin@fledgepractice.com</a>.
