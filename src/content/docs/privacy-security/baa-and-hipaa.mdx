When you use Fledge to manage protected health information (PHI), you and Fledge are in a HIPAA covered-entity / business-associate relationship. That relationship is governed by a Business Associate Agreement (BAA).

## What the BAA covers

In plain language: we promise to handle PHI in line with the HIPAA Security Rule and Privacy Rule, to notify you if there's ever a breach, and to limit our use of the data to what's needed to provide you the service.

## How you sign it

The BAA is presented during onboarding and you accept it electronically. A countersigned copy is available on request — email <a href="mailto:admin@fledgepractice.com">admin@fledgepractice.com</a>.

## Sub-processors

We use a small number of HIPAA-eligible sub-processors (AWS for hosting, Stripe for payment processing). Each one has a BAA in place with us. The current list lives in our public BAA and is updated when it changes.
