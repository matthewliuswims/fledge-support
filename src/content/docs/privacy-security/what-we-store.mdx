This article is the plain-language version of our privacy posture. The legal version lives in our Privacy Policy and the Business Associate Agreement (BAA) we sign with each provider.

## What we store

- **Account data.** Your name, email, password hash, and the practice details you enter at sign-up.
- **Roster.** The clients you add — names, contact details, intake fields you choose to collect.
- **Sessions and notes.** Calendar entries and the clinical notes you attach to them.
- **Invoices and payments.** Amounts, dates, statuses. Card numbers themselves are stored by our payment processor (Stripe), not by Fledge.

## Where it lives

All of the above is stored in AWS, in the United States, in a HIPAA-eligible configuration covered by our BAA with AWS. Encrypted at rest, encrypted in transit. No data is shipped to third-party analytics, ad networks, or AI training providers.

## Who can access it

You. Anyone you grant access to inside your practice (when team accounts ship). Our on-call engineer if you ask us to look at a problem in your account, in which case we touch only what's necessary to diagnose and we log the access.
