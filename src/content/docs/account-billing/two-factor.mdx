Two-factor authentication adds a one-time code to your sign-in flow, on top of your password. We strongly recommend turning it on — your account holds protected health information and the password alone is not enough.

To enable it, open **Settings** → **Security** and follow the prompts to register an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, 1Password, Authy, etc.).

We're writing the longer guide (recovery codes, replacing a lost device, what happens if your authenticator app is on a phone you no longer have). If you're locked out today, email <a href="mailto:admin@fledgepractice.com">admin@fledgepractice.com</a> from the email address on your account and we can help you recover access.
