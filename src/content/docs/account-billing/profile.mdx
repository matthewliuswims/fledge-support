---
title: Updating your profile
description: How to change your name, email, and contact details on your Fledge account.
sidebar:
  order: 1
---

Open **Settings** from the sidebar and pick the **Profile** tab. From there you can update your display name, contact email, phone, and practice details.

Changing the email address you sign in with takes an extra confirmation step (we send a code to the new address) — that's a feature, not a bug, since it's the same address that receives password resets and security notifications.

We're writing the longer guide. For anything that the UI won't let you change today, email <a href="mailto:admin@fledgepractice.com">admin@fledgepractice.com</a>.
