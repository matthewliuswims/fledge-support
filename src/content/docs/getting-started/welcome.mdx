Fledge is a practice-management tool built specifically for speech-language pathologists in private practice. This support hub walks through how to use it.

## What's in the dashboard

- **Today.** A snapshot of your day: upcoming sessions, unpaid invoices, anything that needs attention.
- **Clients.** The roster, with intake details and a per-client history.
- **Schedule.** Calendar of upcoming and past sessions.
- **Invoices.** Issue, send, and track payments.
- **Reports.** Revenue and caseload at a glance.
- **Settings.** Profile, billing, two-factor, integrations.

## What this hub covers

We're writing these articles incrementally. If you can't find what you need, email <a href="mailto:admin@fledgepractice.com">admin@fledgepractice.com</a> — a real person will respond and we'll add the answer here so the next person finds it faster.

## What this hub will never include

This is a public site. We will never publish client names, session content, or anything else that could identify the people you serve. If you need help with something specific to a client, please describe the problem in general terms in your email — we don't need (and don't want) the protected details.
