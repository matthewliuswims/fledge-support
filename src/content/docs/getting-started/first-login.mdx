After you confirm your email during sign-up, head to <a href="https://app.fledgepractice.com/login">app.fledgepractice.com/login</a> and sign in with the email and password you chose.

We're writing this article. In the meantime, if you hit a snag at sign-in — confirmation email didn't arrive, password reset isn't working, two-factor prompt looks wrong — email <a href="mailto:admin@fledgepractice.com">admin@fledgepractice.com</a> with a description of what you're seeing and we'll get you in.
