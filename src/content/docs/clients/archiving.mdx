When you stop seeing a client, archive their record rather than deleting it — your records-retention obligations under state law and professional ethics typically run several years past the last date of service.

Archived clients:

- Don't appear in the active roster or scheduling pickers.
- Stay searchable from the **Clients** page when you toggle "Show archived."
- Keep their full session history, invoices, and notes.
